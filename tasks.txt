Activities to be carried out

Exercise 1 -

Use a different colour format to read/transform the image (Example: COLOR_BGR2BGRA,COLOR_BGR2HSV_FULL, etc.)

Exercise 2 -

You can generate your own hues-to-frequency mapping (hint: sinewaves are used in the example)

Exercise 3 -

Then add noise to the image and identify the sound the noise makes.
